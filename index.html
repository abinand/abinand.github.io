About Me

Hello! I'm Abinand Sivakumar.

I am driven by the love of creating and presenting technical visuals that breathe life into concepts, stories and ideas.

I specialize in crafting visuals that make complex systems intuitive and compelling. Whether building 3D graphics interfaces and games, exploring generative art with p5.js, experimenting with shaders, mapping and documenting processes through UML and BPMN, or data storytelling through dashboards, I focus on clarity, structure, and visual storytelling.

My work lives at the crossroads of math, physics, computer science, and art.

Available for freelance collaborations and contract opportunities.
